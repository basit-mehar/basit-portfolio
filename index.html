About Me

Building Intelligent Systems

I am an AI/ML Engineer focused on building intelligent systems using Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and FastAPI. My work centers on developing Agentic AI systems and scalable AI-powered applications that combine research with practical, production-ready solutions.

I have hands-on experience creating AI systems for deepfake detection, media authenticity verification, and intelligent automation. My approach integrates machine learning models, neural networks, and modern backend architectures to build reliable and scalable AI services.

I am the Founder of Forensica AI, a platform designed to detect deepfake videos, manipulated images, AI-generated text, synthetic voice, and misleading news content, helping verify the authenticity of digital media.

I am also the Founder of AdNova, an AI-powered marketing agent that uses intelligent automation to help businesses optimize campaigns and make data-driven marketing decisions.

I am passionate about transforming innovative AI ideas into real-world products and continuously exploring new advancements in Agentic AI, machine learning systems, and AI infrastructure.

Mission

Building Trustworthy AI

My mission is to build trustworthy and impactful AI systems that solve real-world problems and improve how humans interact with technology.

At the same time, through AdNova, I explore how Agentic AI can automate complex workflows, helping businesses leverage artificial intelligence for smarter and more efficient decision-making.

I believe the future of technology lies in responsible AI, intelligent automation, and scalable AI infrastructure, and my goal is to build products that push these boundaries while creating meaningful impact.
